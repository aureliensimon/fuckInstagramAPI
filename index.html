Where to find the url ?

To find the url of the photo, go to the profile who own the photo and select the one you want to download. Then click on the 3 dots on the top right corner. You will see a dropdown menu and chose the 'Copy Link' one. Once you have click on it, the link will be automatically copy to the clipboard so you just have to paste it in the input overhead !
